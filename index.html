Behind the Bagels

Mr. J's Bagels & Deli opened in 1990 after owners Joey and Dave Jerlinski came to town to visit their cousin, who always wanted them to bring New York bagels down with them. When they came, they saw the need for bagels in Harrisonburg! Now, Mr. J's has three locations and a large variety of bagels and food items on the menu. With fifteen different flavors of bagels, not including seasonal specialty flavors, Mr. J's is a favorite in the community for bagels and deli sandwiches. Offering a variety of catering dishes, Mr. J's also supplies the area with an outlet for serving a large group of people at a fair price!
